About BECON

BECON (Bengal Engineering Conclave of Nations) is the flagship Model United Nations conference organised by The Debating Society of IIEST Shibpur.

Step into the world of diplomacy, lobbying, geopolitics and high-stakes negotiation where every delegate holds the power to influence global narratives.

When silence is easy, will you dare to speak?
